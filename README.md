The dashboard focuses on transaction performance, user behavior, service-wise contribution, payment success rates, and customer segmentation. Along with visualizing data, the project emphasizes business storytelling and insight generation to support data-driven decision-making.

For me, this project was also an opportunity to revisit and strengthen my Power BI skills. While working on it, I improved my understanding of DAX, KPI development, dashboard design, and custom tooltips. Every visual, KPI, measure, and analysis in this dashboard was built independently as part of my learning journey toward becoming a Data Analyst.

## Project Objective
The objective of this project was to transform raw transaction data into actionable insights and answer key business questions:

- How are transactions performing over time?
- Which services contribute the highest transaction value?
- Which customer segments drive platform usage?
- How reliable is the payment ecosystem?
- What transaction patterns can be observed across different user groups?

## Key Insights
---->Loan Services Generate the Highest Transaction Value
Loan-related transactions contribute approximately ₹2.5 Billion, making them the most valuable service category on the platform.

## Strong Payment Reliability
The dashboard indicates a payment success rate of 96%, reflecting a highly reliable transaction ecosystem.

## Weekdays Drive Most Platform Activity
Around 71.6% of all transactions occur during weekdays, while weekend activity contributes 28.4%.

## Millennials and Gen X Are the Most Active Segments
Millennials and Gen X together account for nearly 75% of platform usage, making them the primary customer segments.

##High-Value Users Have Significant Impact
The top users generate transaction values exceeding one million, highlighting the importance of customer retention strategies.

##Positive Growth Trend
Both transaction volume and transaction value show healthy month-over-month growth.

## Business Recommendations
- Continue investing in loan-related services.
- Introduce weekend offers to increase transaction activity.
- Focus marketing efforts on Millennials and Gen X users.
- Develop loyalty programs for premium customers.
- Maintain transaction reliability through continuous monitoring.
